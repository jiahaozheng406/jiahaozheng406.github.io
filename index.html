Call me ++: Jiahao sounds like Plus Sign in Chinese, and Zheng means positive. For me, ++ means learning a little more than others every day and continuously surpassing myself.

My research interests focus on 3D Vision, Multimodal Large Language Models, and World Models. I began with physics-aware sensing and signal processing, then extended constraint-guided learning to sparse-view 3D reconstruction, Real2Sim, editable CAD generation, and embodied systems.

I am actively applying for PhD opportunities for Fall 2027 and Spring/Fall 2028. I hope to study how multimodal models and world models can perceive, understand, generate, and interact with complex three-dimensional environments.

SegGeoGSSparse-View 3DGS

Towards Robust Sparse-View 3D Gaussian Splatting via Hierarchical Depth and Multi-View Consistency

Zheng Jianhui, Jiahao Zheng, Huo Zhiyong†

ICMR '26: Proceedings of the 2026 International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval, pp. 1120–1127

SegGeoGS reformulates sparse-view 3D Gaussian Splatting as a hierarchical consensus problem across geometric and photometric manifolds. It combines semantic foreground constraints, adaptive depth-prior calibration, stochastic Gaussian depth rendering, and multi-view geometric/texture consistency to improve geometry fidelity under sparse observations.

NV-ThermoPhysics-aware ST Learning

Diamond thermometry beyond the zero-phonon line via physics-aware spectral trend learning

Jiahao Zheng, Shu Liu, Weizhou Wu, Jinxu Wang, Junchi Gao, Tianxiang Li, Jingqiang Han, Xiaoya Xu, Chenlu Bu, Baihao Zhou, Yiming Jia, Yinjun Wang, Zixuan Tan, Xinran Gao, Jiayi Yuan, Hui Yang, and Guanxiang Du†

Applied Physics Letters, 129(1): 014001, 2026 · Nature Index

This work moves diamond thermometry beyond fragile zero-phonon-line peak tracking by learning the thermodynamic evolution of the phonon sideband over a broad spectral window. A physics-aware spectral decomposition and five-channel dilated ResNet enable robust microwave-free temperature inference under high-temperature and low-SNR conditions.

NV-PulseU-Net Reconstruction

Real-time on-chip nanosecond pulse capture with NV centers: U-Net denoising and signal reconstruction

Honghao Chen, Haoyu Li, Jiahao Zheng, Youwei Liu, Haoran Zhou, Yinjun Wang, Chenxin Zhang, Jinxu Wang, Na Li, Aiqing Chen, Jingcheng Huang, Baihao Zhou, and Guanxiang Du†

Chinese Physics B, Accepted Manuscript online 15 April 2026 · DOI: 10.1088/1674-1056/ae5f88

This accepted manuscript studies real-time on-chip nanosecond pulse capture with diamond NV centers, using U-Net denoising and signal reconstruction to recover transient sensing signals from noisy measurements.
